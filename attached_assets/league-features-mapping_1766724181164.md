## Content Flow Examples

### Example 1: Finding a Pickleball League
**User Journey:**

1. **Option A - Browse:**
   - Land on Discover
   - Click Pickleball sport icon
   - See featured pickleball leagues
   - Click league â†’ View details â†’ Join

2. **Option B - Search:**
   - Click Search
   - Click "Leagues" tab
   - Filter: Pickleball, Tuesday, Intermediate
   - See results â†’ Click league â†’ Join

**Result:** Clear paths for both casual browsing and intentional searching

---

### Example 2: Checking When My Next Game Is
**User Journey:**

1. Click My Leagues
2. Click "My Schedule" section
3. See all upcoming games across all sports
4. Click game for details

**Alternative:**
1. Click My Leagues
2. Click specific league
3. See that league's schedule

**Result:** Flexible access, aggregated or league-specific

---

### Example 3: Venue Manager Posts New League
**User Journey:**

1. Click My Leagues
2. Click "+ Create League"
3. Fill in league details
4. Click "Post to Bulletin Board" option
5. League appears:
   - In Search > Leagues (searchable)
   - On Discover feed (announcement post)
   - Notifications to interested users

**Result:** One creation flow, multiple distribution channels
