## Page Overview

**URL:** `/for-venues`  
**Goal:** Convert venue managers to sign up for paid subscription ($99-299/month)  
**Target Audience:** Golf courses, bowling alleys, sports complexes, tennis clubs, pickleball facilities, softball fields

**Key Metrics:**
- Time on page
- Scroll depth
- CTA click rate
- Sign-up conversion rate
- Contact sales requests
